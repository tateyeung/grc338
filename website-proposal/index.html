Home

Referring to the halcyon days of tea, Halcyon's mission is to deliver teas reminiscent of an idyllic time of pure ingredients.

Bubble tea has evolved many 'generations' from when it was prepared with tapioca balls sitting a bucket of syrup for days at the bottom of mom-and-pop Vietnamese cafes with pre-prepared teas.

The earliest specialized bubble tea shops and the explosion of franchised chains and mass-produced artificial syrups cemented bubble tea as a mainstay in America, and recently 'innovative' boutiques have invested in mixology.

We believe the new generation is here.

This generation of bubble tea prepares tapioca pearls in small batches and uses nothing artificial in the drinks — only premium leaves, real creams and milks, and natural flavorings.
